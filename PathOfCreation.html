Description

The Path Of Creation is an intense puzzle and 3D platforms game of minimalist style, with a lot of symbology and a personal story. Enter this abstract world to overcome its challenges and try to unravel its secrets. Make use of the destruction and physics of this surreal world to open your own path.

Features

  • Destroy certain parts of the map in your favor to overcome each level in different ways.
  • A clean and unique minimalist style.
  • Try to unravel this little personal story.
  • Discover its true ending.

History

In August of 2017 JuanJose Gomez, an 18-year-old boy after making several prototypes and experiments, decided to make a game, but this time he wanted to show his work to everyone. The idea came after experimenting with the physics provided by the Unreal Engine 4. After several prototypes and a year and a half later this little game has been finished.

About Juan José Gómez

Juan José Gómez is an independent developer who started learning about videogame programming in August of 2016 with 17 years. Started with RPGMaker, followed by GameMaker, Unity and finally made the jump to Unreal Engine 4.With this jump Juan José decided to do his next serious project, but this time he wanted to show it to everyone and that project was called The Path Of Creation , his first video game.
